THE DISTRICT

This is a further division of the Area which is headed by a Senior Pastor or a Pastor. The District Pastor administers the district with the assistance of the District Executive Committee Members. The highest decision-making body is the District Presbytery. The District Presbytery Council is made up of the District Pastor and his Wife All Pastors / Overseer, One Pastorâ€™s Wife Representative, The District Executives, Two (2) Elders representatives, two (2) Deacons and Deaconesses and two (2) Prophets / Prophetesses in the District. The District Presbytery, which is the highest policy-making body in the District, decides on all matters affecting the growth, development and welfare of the Local congregations. It also receives and approves reports submitted by the various organs of the Church in the District.
