THE LOCAL CONGREGATION

This is a further division of the district and forms the base of the administrative structure of the Church. The local congregation is headed by a Local Pastor or Overseer who sees to the implementation of decisions taken by the District Pastor, with the aid of the Presiding Elder, all Elders at the local assembly, all Deacons and Deaconesses in the local congregation. This body make up the Local Presbytery Council which is the coordinating and highest decision making body at the local assembly. Among other functions of the Local Presbytery Council; to discuss and decide and report on matters affecting the assembly and be responsible to the District Presbytery Council. This well-coordinated chain of command ensures both vertical and horizontal flow of information in the Church, thereby making implementation of policies and feedback very vital ingredients for effective administration.
